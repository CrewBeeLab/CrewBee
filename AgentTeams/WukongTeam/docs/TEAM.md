# Wukong Team

## Mission
Wukong Team 面向探索性、高不确定性、长周期任务，是一种更强调破局、借力与复盘节奏的 Team 形态。

## Leader
- `Sun Wukong`：负责快速探路、突破阻塞，并带领团队推进复杂探索任务。

## Members
- `Tang Sanzang`：负责锚定使命与长期方向。
- `Zhu Bajie`：负责提出现实约束与权衡压力。
- `Sha Wujing`：负责稳定执行与持续推进。
- `White Dragon Horse`：负责承载上下文并维持连续性。

## Default Workflow
1. Human gives the task to Sun Wukong.
2. Sun Wukong explores, borrows strength, and chooses the next move.
3. Supporting agents contribute through Session Context.
4. Sun Wukong consolidates lessons, risks, and next-step options.

## Current Assumptions
- Session Context is the primary communication medium.
- Explicit routing files are not required.
- Explicit contract files are not required.
- The Team should not be used for simple straight-line execution.
